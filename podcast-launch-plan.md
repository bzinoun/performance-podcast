## ⚠️ Launch Risks & Mitigations

| Risk | Mitigation |
|---|---|
| Low initial downloads | Seed with personal network first |
| Guest cancels recording | Have 2 backup guests at all times |
| Audio quality poor | Record in studio, test before each session |
| No reviews | DM 20 close contacts with direct link |
| Inconsistent publishing | Batch record 2 episodes before launch |

---

## 🗓️ Publishing Cadence

- **Preferred:** Every 2 weeks
- **Minimum:** 1 episode per month
- **Batch recording:** Record 2 episodes in one session when possible
- **Content calendar:** Always have 2 episodes scheduled ahead
