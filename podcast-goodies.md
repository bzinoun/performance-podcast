# PERFORMANCE Podcast — Goodies & Guest Gifts

## Philosophy
When a guest records with us, they deserve more than a podcast mention. We want them to leave feeling valued — and to genuinely want to share the show with their network. Goodies are the physical/digital proof that we take this seriously.

---

## 🎁 For Recording Day / Post-Recording Package

### The "PERFORMANCE Kit" — sent after recording confirmation

Every guest receives a package after recording:

**Physical:**
- 🎧 PERFORMANCE branded headphones/earbuds case (custom)
- 🧢 PERFORMANCE cap (premium embroidered)
- 🏷️ PERFORMANCE t-shirt (premium cotton, sizes M/L/XL — ask beforehand)
- 📇 Branded notebook (Moleskine-style) — "Notes from the field"
- 🏅 PERFORMANCE lapel pin (Collector's item)

**Digital:**
- 📧 High-res episode cover with guest's name
- 📱 Social media assets (quote cards, episode graphic)
- 🎙️ Clean audio file of their episode (for their own use)
- 🔗 Personalized referral link for the episode

**Personal touch:**
- Handwritten thank-you note
- Photo from recording day (if in-person)
- Their Strava achievement badge (if they have one, frame it)

## 💡 Experience-Based Gifts (most impactful)

### High-value gestures
- **Register them for a race** (their next goal race — pay entry fee)
- **Training session together** — join them for a run/ride in Casablanca

- **Introduction to another guest** — facilitate the network
- **VIP access to a future PERFORMANCE live event**
- **Free session with a coach / sports nutritionist they admire**

## 🌍 Environmental / Ethical Choices

- Source locally when possible (Casablanca printers, Moroccan cotton)
- Use recycled materials for packaging
- Avoid single-use plastic
- Include a handwritten card (not printed) — personal matters
- Consider offsetting carbon for shipped items
